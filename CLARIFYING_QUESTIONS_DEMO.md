# Clarifying Questions Feature Demo

## How It Works

The MedSentry AI system now includes clarifying questions alongside medication suggestions and medical reports to provide a more comprehensive and interactive user experience.

## Example Scenario

### User Input:
"I have a headache and fever"

### System Response:
The AI analyzes the symptoms and provides:

1. **Clinical Analysis**
   - Triage Level: Medium
   - Summary: Your symptoms of headache and fever could indicate several conditions ranging from a common cold to more serious infections.

2. **Need More Information?** (Clarifying Questions)
   - How long have you had these symptoms?
   - What is your temperature reading?
   - Do you have any other symptoms like neck stiffness or rash?

3. **Suggested Medications**
   - **Suggested Medication:** Acetaminophen (Tylenol)
   - **Use Case:** For fever and mild to moderate pain relief
   - **Typical Dosage:** Adults: 500-1000mg every 6 hours (max 4000mg/day)
   - **Important Notes:** Safe for most people when used as directed. Avoid alcohol. Not for children under 2 years.
   - **⚠️ Warning:** Always consult healthcare provider before use, especially if you have liver disease or take other medications

4. **Treatment Plan**
   - Monitor temperature every 4 hours
   - Stay hydrated with clear fluids
   - Rest in a cool, comfortable environment

## User Interaction Flow

1. User enters symptoms
2. System displays initial analysis with clarifying questions
3. User answers clarifying questions
4. System provides refined analysis with more specific medication suggestions
5. User can continue the conversation for further clarification

## Benefits

- **Enhanced Accuracy:** Gathering more information leads to better assessments
- **Interactive Experience:** Users are guided through the process
- **Educational Value:** Users learn what information is important for their condition
- **Comprehensive Care:** Medication suggestions are provided alongside medical analysis
